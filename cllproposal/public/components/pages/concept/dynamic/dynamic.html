Create a new Concept

Follow these steps to create your Concept
  • Details A concept is a basic entity in the system. It groups clinical codes to help define a single event or condition within the library. Provide us details about your concept to continue.
  • Publication Give the user some information regarding publication, source reference(s) and citation requirements.
  • Validation Give the user some information regarding validation, and describe what type of validation they may have performed.
  • Permissions Permissions define who can access and edit your concept. You can assign permissions specific to the owner, groups, as well as everyone else.
  • Rules Each concept is composed of rules, these rules describe which codes are included or excluded from the final Concept's codelist. You can add new codes, or import codelists from other concepts when creating your ruleset.
  • Review You can preview your concept before publication. We recommend you review it thoroughly before publication.
